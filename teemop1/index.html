History

TeemOP1 was formed in Summer 2013 for the Amazon League of Legends internal tournament hosted by RiotGames. Although this team is fairly new, the roster includes players from Beta release of the game in 2009. The team name 'TeemOP1' is a reference to Teemo, one of the game's most loved and hated playable characters.
