Atlas Clothing offers an ever-changing selection of vintage clothing for the discerning buyer. Our employees hand select each piece to reflect the classic as well as the current. We pride ourselves on providing beautiful pieces that will add to anyone's wardrobe. Atlas has been in the heart of capital hill for more than two decades and continues to be at the forefront of vintage fashion.

Our History

Atlas Co. back then exported trendy clothes to Japan. In the past decade, Atlas Clothing has changed its main purpose from exporting to selling local goods due to the decreased interest of American clothing trends in Japan. They presently engage in e-commerce through sites such as ebay and Etsy; planning to create their e-commerce site to boost sales and reduce costs. They advertise through fashion shows in their store to catch local attention. Atlas currently is part of Fremont Vintage Mall store. While Fremont Vintage Mall sell diverse selection of products, Atlas is focusing on the clothing section. Another way Atlas exposes its brand is by selling clothing at Fremont Vintage Mall where other vintage goods are also sold.
